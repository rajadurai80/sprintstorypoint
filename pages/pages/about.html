About Us

Empowering agile teams with intuitive tools designed to optimize workflows, enhance collaboration, and drive continuous improvement.

Our Mission

To empower agile teams with intuitive, effective tools that streamline project management processes, facilitate collaboration, and drive continuous improvement. We believe in the power of agile practices to transform how teams work, and our goal is to make these practices accessible and easy to implement.

Our Story

Founded by an experienced agile practitioner and software developer, Sprint Story Point was born out of a need for better tools to support agile teams. With years of experience working in agile environments and facing common challenges, we created solutions that address these issues head-on.

What started as a simple planning poker tool has evolved into a comprehensive suite of agile tools, all designed with one goal in mind: making agile practices accessible, efficient, and enjoyable for teams of all sizes.

Our Commitment

We are committed to continuous improvement and always looking for ways to enhance our tools and services. We value feedback from users and believe that collaboration is key to innovation.

Whether you're a small startup or a large enterprise, Sprint Story Point is here to support your agile journey. Thank you for choosing us. We're excited to be a part of your agile transformation.
