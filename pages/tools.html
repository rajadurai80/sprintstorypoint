# Tool Description
1 Retrospective Board A digital board where team members can add their thoughts on what went well, what didn't, and how to improve. Include features for voting and categorizing feedback.
2 Burndown Chart Generator A tool to automatically generate burndown charts from sprint data, showing progress towards the sprint goal.
3 Kanban Board An interactive Kanban board with drag-and-drop functionality, customizable columns, and integration with other project management tools.
4 Story Mapping Tool A visual tool for creating and managing user story maps, helping teams understand the user journey and prioritize work.
5 Capacity Planning Tool A tool for planning team capacity based on availability, skill sets, and historical velocity, to help in sprint planning.
6 Daily Standup Bot A bot that integrates with chat platforms like Slack or Teams, prompting team members for their updates and summarizing them for the team.
7 Automated Velocity Tracker A tool that calculates team velocity over multiple sprints, identifying trends and helping in future sprint planning.
8 Dependency Tracker A tool to track dependencies between tasks or stories, visualizing potential bottlenecks and helping manage cross-team dependencies.
9 Agile Metrics Dashboard A customizable dashboard displaying key agile metrics such as cycle time, lead time, sprint burndown, and team velocity.
10 Estimation Poker Tool An online version of Planning Poker, allowing remote teams to estimate story points collaboratively.
11 Risk Management Tool A tool for identifying, assessing, and tracking risks throughout the sprint, with options for mitigation strategies.
12 Release Planning Tool A tool to help teams plan and track releases, including feature tracking, release notes, and timeline visualization.
13 Team Health Check A survey tool for conducting regular health checks on team morale, engagement, and productivity, with analytics to identify trends and areas for improvement.
14 Agile Training Modules Interactive training modules for new team members to learn agile practices, scrum roles, and best practices.
15 Retrospective Action Tracker A tool for tracking actions decided in retrospectives, assigning owners, setting deadlines, and following up on progress.
